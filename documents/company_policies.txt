COMPANY POLICIES AND PROCEDURES
Document Version: 2.1
Effective Date: January 2024

VACATION POLICY
All full-time employees are entitled to 15 paid vacation days per year. Vacation days accrue at a rate of 1.25 days per month. Employees must submit vacation requests at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal. Vacation approval is subject to business needs and team coverage.

SICK LEAVE POLICY
Employees receive 10 paid sick days annually. Unused sick days do not roll over to the next year. For illness exceeding 3 consecutive days, a doctor's note is required.

REMOTE WORK POLICY
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Remote work days must be scheduled in advance. Employees must maintain a professional workspace and be available during core business hours (9 AM - 5 PM EST).

EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
All business expenses must be pre-approved using the expense request form. Receipts must be submitted within 30 days of purchase. Allowable expenses include client meals, business travel, and required software tools.

WORKING HOURS
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Flexible scheduling is available with manager approval. Overtime must be pre-approved and is compensated at 1.5x hourly rate.

DRESS CODE
Business casual attire is required Monday through Thursday. Casual dress is permitted on Fridays. Client meetings require business professional attire.

CONFIDENTIALITY AGREEMENT
All employees must sign confidentiality agreements. Company information, client data, and proprietary processes must not be shared externally without authorization.
