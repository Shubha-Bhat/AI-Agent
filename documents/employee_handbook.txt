EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK
Tech Solutions Inc.

ONBOARDING PROCESS
New employees undergo a 2-week onboarding program. This includes system setup, security training, and department introductions. All new hires are assigned a mentor for the first 90 days.

PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
Performance reviews are conducted quarterly. The process includes self-assessment, manager evaluation, and goal setting. Employees can access their performance dashboard through the HR system.

TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT
The company offers $2,000 annual training budget per employee. Approved training includes certifications, conferences, and skill development courses. Employees must submit training requests 30 days in advance.

HEALTHCARE BENEFITS
Medical, dental, and vision insurance available. Company covers 80% of premium costs. Open enrollment occurs every November. New employees have 30 days to enroll after start date.

RETIREMENT PLAN
401(k) plan with 4% company match. Employees become vested after 3 years of service. Contribution limits follow IRS guidelines.

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
Promotion cycles occur every 6 months. Criteria include performance metrics, skill development, and leadership potential. Employees can request career path discussions with HR.

EQUIPMENT POLICY
Company provides laptop, monitor, and necessary software. Employees are responsible for securing company equipment. Lost or damaged equipment must be reported within 24 hours.
